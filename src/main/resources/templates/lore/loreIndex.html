Nakus {i}

Nakus was the god of all gods. He conquered all professions in the ol' mighty sky. All the other gods' mouths were shut while he was talking. They obeyed him. His first program was written back in 1991.

Angelistar {i}

Angelistar was one of Nakus' finest apprentices. Proud and powerful, he defended all his mentor stood for. In this sense, no one who defied programming was left unpunished by the mighty Angelistar.

KarthSimos {i}

A prodigy since birth, Karthsimos was always considered peerles by his mates. He would always do the things others wouldn't even dare. Thus, his traits naturally led him to becoming a programming genius, as well as one of Nakus' chosen warriors at a very young age.

Royammus {i}

A cheesy and well-going being, Royammus's vast experience in programming quickly brought him to becoming one of Nakus' best know warriors. Flexible and adaptive, he is known for always been there where he's needed, helping those in need of programming knowledge.

Geshplank {i}

Though Geshplank was "new" to the nature of programming, only those who didn't know him well would dare call him "new". Having spent countless hours grinding his favourite activity, Geshplank was able to prove himself in front of Nakus faster than any other, which led to him becoming one of the best.

cEkko {i}

cEkko's keystone lay in finding out how to repair (almost) everything that had gone wrong. Having already brought appendages of his own body back from the dead, he was the perfect candidate for discovering bugs and other insects which would cause even the mighties program to fail miserably.

Tryndaminko {i}

Tryndaminko was cEkko's barbarish upstart and companion. Apart from destroying everything he came upon with his blade *cough* mouse *cough*, he also loved destroying huge amounts of drink and food.
